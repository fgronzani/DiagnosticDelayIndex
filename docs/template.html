Diagnostic Delay Index (DDI): Temporal and Regional Analysis Across Clinical Conditions

Abstract: This automated observatory tracks population-level signals of diagnostic delay using aggregate hospital admission severity. By monitoring a composite severity score (weighted by ICU admission, mortality, and length of stay) mapped to the 75th percentile (DDI), we compute statistically robust temporal degradation trajectories and regional disparities across multiple pathologies.
